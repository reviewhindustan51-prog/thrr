About Us

The Hindustan Review

The journal is intended to serve as a forum for discussion and exposition of the various aspects of India and Buddhist learnings, and their applicability in societies of the world.

Certainly, the Journal does not seek to be a vehicle for comments either on political parties and religious forums, their programs and performance or conduct of individuals, however placed. The aim of the Journal is to be objective and informative.

Contributions for the Journal are invited from persons belonging to every walk of life and interested in dimensions of social, political, administrative, religious, cultural, peace and non-violence, literature and so on.

Contributors are requested to send signed copies of their contributions in two sets (hard copy) and by email (soft copy). Publishers desiring review of their publications are requested to send two copies of their publications.

Natural Science Today

Natural Science Today is a peer-reviewed, international and multidisciplinary journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research in all areas of natural sciences including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Environmental Science, Mathematics, Earth Sciences, and emerging interdisciplinary fields.

The journal serves as a platform for the dissemination of cutting-edge scientific research, innovations, and discoveries that contribute to the advancement of scientific knowledge and address contemporary global challenges such as climate change, sustainable development, public health, and technological progress.

We encourage original research papers, review articles, short communications, and scientific reports from researchers, academicians, and scientists across the globe. The journal maintains strict ethical standards, rigorous peer review, and a commitment to scientific accuracy and integrity.

Contributions are invited from scholars engaged in fundamental and applied research. Authors are requested to submit their manuscripts in proper format as per the journal guidelines. Publishers and authors desiring book reviews or special issues may contact the editorial office.

Our Values

  • Academic Integrity: Strict peer-review process and ethical publishing standards.
  • Open Access: Making research freely available to scholars worldwide.
  • Inclusivity: Encouraging contributions from researchers across India and abroad.
  • Timeliness: Ensuring efficient review and publication process.

Publisher Information

Published by Buddhist Centre for Action Research and Development Studies (BCARDS), Patna, Bihar. The organization is committed to educational development and research promotion in eastern India.

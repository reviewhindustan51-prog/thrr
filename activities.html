Governing Document

Activities & Objectives

1. Aims and Objectives

  • The Society shall be established on an all-India basis.
  • Membership shall be open to persons from all states of the country.
  • The problems and topics selected for research and the courses conducted shall cover all parts of the country.
  • The Society shall endeavour to extend its area of activities to all States of India.

2. Objectives of the Society

The objectives for which the Society is formed are to undertake, promote, coordinate and organise scientific, economic, environmental, historical, political, social, cultural, agricultural and rural development programmes. The Society shall also engage in para-medical and nurses training, management and administration, fashion designing and technology, science, engineering and technology, law, computer science, journalism and mass communication, B. Pharma, B. Ed., Library Science, welfare of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, and welfare of weaker sections of society (particularly women and children).

The Society shall generally undertake all activities necessary, conducive, incidental or ancillary to attain the above objects. It shall introduce new courses as per the emerging needs of society and establish and manage institutions for the fulfilment of its objectives.
